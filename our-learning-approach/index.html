Our Learning Approach

A strong educational foundation is crucial to not only determining academic success, but also for life success. Several decades of research demonstrate that high-quality, developmentally appropriate early childhood programs produce short and long-term positive effects on children’s cognitive and social development. During those early years, how that child learns and develops — mentally, emotionally and socially — is critical. At Gisire, we use a unique integrated approach to support our youngest students’ growth, offering opportunities to develop intellectual curiosity and a love of learning.

For our youngest students, our instructional model focuses on students’ passions – we want to make sure that we understand each student at an individual level.

At Gisire, our learning experience ensures that we’re preparing our students to shape and lead their future, not simply work in it

People learn best when we…

  • feel safe, supported and connected to each other
  • have a clear purpose for learning
  • set clear and high expectations
  • engage in rich, relevant and authentic experiences
  • take responsibility and set goals for our own learning
  • collaborate and communicate
  • are challenged and take appropriate risks
  • apply and practice new learning
  • receive on-going, quality feedback
  • reflect on ourselves as learners
